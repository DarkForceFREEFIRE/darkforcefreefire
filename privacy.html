Walker Regedits Website Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8/5/2024


At the xWalker Cheat Team, we respect your privacy. This document shows what information we collect.

Information we collect and store when visiting the website

When you open a blog page, we calculate an md5 hash of your IP address for the view counter. The hash is not viewed or shared.


Data Sharing

We do not share any information with anyone, it stays on our servers.
